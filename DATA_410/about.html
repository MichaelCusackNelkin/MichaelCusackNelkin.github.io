UNDER CONSTRUCTION Advanced Applied Machine Learning Course

Repository for work on my undergraduate data science capstone at W&M. It focuses on an idea for a mobile app that uses a series of recurrent and convolutional neural networks to determine whether a video file contains an idleing commercial truck which can be fined under New York City law. The potential users are the public walking the streets of NYC, as they get a % of any fines levied on the idleing trucks that they tape.
